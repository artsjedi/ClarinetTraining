"Clarinet Practive" is an excellent App for you to train your ability with an clarinet.

1. The application shows a random note in the score
2. The app shows the correct fingering on the clarinet
3. The musician plays the corresponding note.
4. The application plays the sound of the note to train your ear.

Features:
- Shows notes in musical sheet
- Shows Clarinet fingering for each note.
- Train your typing on the clarinet
- Play clarinet notes Sounds
- Notes dictionary
- Select a musical scale
- Select clarinet tranpose (Bb Eb C)
- Select notes range
- Select the time interval between each note
- Set the rotation Clarinet
- Displays the notes name on musical sheet
- Responsive
